Privacy Policy

Kindlepathy values your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle the information processed by our extension.

Data Collected

When you use our extension and click the submit button, the full readable content of the web page is extracted and sent to our servers for your later access. We do not collect any personally identifiable information about you.

Use of Data

Extracted website content is stored so you can access and read it at your convenience. We do not use this data for analytics, marketing, advertising, or profiling.

Data Retention

Saved articles are stored indefinitely unless you request deletion. You can delete individual articles or all your saved content by contacting us.

User Control

To delete your saved articles, please contact us at egemengol@gmail.com with your request. We will process deletions within 30 days.

Data Location

Your saved content is stored on secure servers. Data may be processed and stored in various locations to ensure service reliability.

Data Sharing

We do not share your saved content or any associated data with third parties.

Data Security

We apply reasonable security measures to protect the stored website content. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted with an updated date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at egemengol@gmail.com.
